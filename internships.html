Available Internship Positions

Multiple opportunities for students and graduates

Laboratory Technician Intern

Full-time / Part-time

Duration: 3-6 months

Requirements: B.Sc. in Biotechnology/Botany/Agriculture or related field

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in tissue culture operations
  • Maintain laboratory equipment and records
  • Support quality control processes
  • Participate in plant development projects

Research & Development Intern

Full-time

Duration: 6-12 months

Requirements: M.Sc. in Biotechnology/Agriculture or pursuing Ph.D.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct research on plant genetics and breeding
  • Develop new plant varieties
  • Analyze experimental data
  • Document research findings

Field Operations Intern

Full-time

Duration: 3-6 months

Requirements: B.Sc. in Agriculture/Horticulture or related field

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in field trials and plant evaluation
  • Monitor plant growth and health
  • Collect and record field data
  • Support farm management activities

Marketing & Sales Intern

Part-time

Duration: 3-4 months

Requirements: BBA/MBA or related marketing/business field

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in marketing campaigns
  • Customer relationship management
  • Market research and analysis
  • Support sales activities
