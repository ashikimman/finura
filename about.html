Our Story

Finura Agro Tech LLP is promoted by Finura at Thovalai. We work hard to provide excellent customer service to all our clients.

FINURA AGRO TECH LLP is a Limited Liability Partnership firm located in 11/8J Finura Garden, Loyola College Road, Thovalai and Nanguneri, Tirunelveli District, Tamilnadu, India. It is registered at Registrar of Companies, Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Govt of India.

Designated Partners of Finura Agro Tech LLP are Mr. M. Ashok Macrin, Deputy Director of Horticulture (Retd) and Mrs. Anisha Shainnie Thangam, Former Professor of Scott Christian College, Nagercoil. Finura Agro Tech LLP's LLP Identification Number is (LLPIN) AAY-4389. Registered on 3rd September 2021. Its registered office is sited at 2F/1A West Lutheran Street, Nagercoil, Kanyakumari District, Tamilnadu, India.

Finura Agro Tech LLP is a leading company in the field of organic farming and biotechnology, located in Kanniyakumari. We offer a range of organic products, research, lab and consultation services to help you grow healthy and sustainable crops.

Our Divisions

Comprehensive services in organic farming and biotechnology

1. Tissue Culture Unit

We produce high-quality and disease-free plantlets using advanced tissue culture techniques, ensuring genetic uniformity and superior plant quality.

2. Training & Research

We provide training and research opportunities for farmers, students, and researchers on various aspects of organic farming and biotechnology.

3. Organic Inputs

We supply organic inputs, such as biofertilizers, biopesticides, vermicompost, and organic seeds, to enhance soil fertility and crop productivity.

4. Nursery

We have a well-maintained nursery, where we grow and sell a variety of plants, such as fruits, vegetables, flowers, herbs, and medicinal plants.

5. Mushroom Production Unit

We cultivate and sell different types of mushrooms, such as oyster, button, milky, and ganoderma, which are rich in protein and nutrients.

6. Soil Testing Lab

We conduct soil testing and analysis to determine the soil quality and nutrient status, and provide recommendations for soil improvement and management.

7. Microbiology Lab

We conduct microbiological tests and studies to identify and isolate beneficial microorganisms, such as bacteria, fungi, and algae, for various applications.

8. Consultation

We offer consultation services for farmers, entrepreneurs, and organizations who want to start or improve their organic farming and biotechnology projects.
